# Ashia Portal: Feature Summary & Product Overview

As the Product Manager for **Ashia Portal**, I am excited to present the core features of our comprehensive Health Insurance Management Ecosystem. Ashia Portal is designed to bridge the gap between residents, healthcare providers, and the State Health Insurance Agency, ensuring seamless access to affordable healthcare through a digital-first approach.

---

## 🏗️ Core Ecosystem Modules

### 1. Unified Enrollee Experience
*   **Self-Service Registration**: Residents can easily enroll in the health scheme from the comfort of their homes using our intuitive web interface.
*   **Family Portfolio Management**: Enrollees can add and manage dependants (spouses and children), ensuring the entire family is covered under a single account.
*   **Digital Identity Management**: Our system generates digital ID cards within 24 hours of registration, providing immediate proof of coverage at healthcare facilities.
*   **Automated Engagement**: Integrated SMS notifications for registration milestones and personalized updates like birthday greetings.

### 2. Healthcare Provider (HCP) Excellence
*   **Smart Facility Directory**: A comprehensive list of accredited healthcare providers, searchable by location (LGA/Town) and specialty.
*   **Digital Encounter Tracking**: HCPs can record patient visits in real-time, creating a transparent record of care and visitation.
*   **Inter-Facility Referrals**: Streamlined referral workflows that allow primary care providers to refer patients to secondary or tertiary facilities with complete medical context.

### 3. Prior Authorization (PA) & Claims Engine
*   **Real-Time PA Requests**: Eliminates administrative delays by allowing providers to submit and track Prior Authorization requests for specialized services.
*   **Electronic Claims Submission**: Providers can submit claims digitally, reducing paperwork and accelerating the reimbursement cycle.
*   **Transparent Processing**: Track claim status from submission through vetting to final payout approval.

### 4. Robust Financial Infrastructure
*   **Secure Remita Integration**: Integrated with Remita for seamless payment of premiums via RRR generation, bank transfers, or card payments.
*   **Bulk Payout Systems**: Automated batch processing for provider payments, allowing for efficient disbursement of funds.
*   **Expense & Budget Governance**: Internal workflows for managing agency expenses, including multi-level approval stages for financial transparency.
*   **Accountant’s Command Center**: Dedicated tools for reconciliation, invoice tracking, and financial reporting.

### 5. Data-Driven Governance & Administration
*   **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Secure access for various stakeholders including Registration Officers, Claims Processors, Accountants, and Super Admins.
*   **Comprehensive Audit Logs**: Every action within the portal is logged, ensuring complete accountability and transparency across the agency.
*   **Dynamic Reporting Suite**: Generate daily, monthly, and yearly reports on enrollment trends, facility performance, and financial health at the State and LGA levels.
*   **Internal Messaging**: A secure, built-in communication channel for agency staff to collaborate on enrollee issues and policy management.

### 6. Public Engagement & Education
*   **Information Hub**: A dedicated section for the latest news, updates, and policy changes from the Anambra State Health Insurance Agency.
*   **Video Resources**: A gallery of educational videos to help users understand the scheme and how to maximize their benefits.
*   **24/7 Support**: Integration with online support channels to assist users at any time of the day.

Ashia Portal isn't just a database; it’s a **transparency engine**. By digitizing the end-to-end journey—from the moment a resident registers to the final payout to a hospital—we ensure that healthcare remains **affordable, accessible, and accountable** for every citizen.
